IOT BASED MONITORING AND AUTOMATED WATERING SYSTEM FOR RESIDENTIAL PLANTS USING ARDUINO MEGA

Utilizes the Arduino Mega microcontroller to provide a comprehensive solution for plant care. The system employs various sensors to monitor crucial environmental parameters such as soilmoisture, and solenoid valve. By leveraging IoT technology, cultivators can remotely monitor and control the watering process through a web-based application.
